Sequencing recommendation

Quarter 1 — earn trust

Ship the cheap-but-loud differentiators: place provenance, export-everything, EXIF photo verification, hyperlocal categories, public utility finder, no-ads pledge. These prove the project's character without big engineering bets.

Quarter 2 — unlock loops

SEO-indexable place pages, embeddable widgets, @handle profiles, email digests, expiring live-trip links, MCP server. Each one is a growth loop that compounds.

Quarter 3 — make a moat

Pick one breakthrough: E2EE places, AI travelogue, map rooms, offline routing, or pilgrimage multi-day mode. Becomes the headline that defines what UMNAAPP is.

Anchor positioning. The landing page today describes UMNAAPP as "Map-first · Real-time · Secure". After picking a moat above, rewrite that line to claim a category — for example "The open, multiplayer, India-first map" or "The map for the rest of your world". The catalog is a means; positioning is the end.
